This report compares U.S. and global energy supply, demand, and prices between December and November 2016 forecasts. It covers petroleum, natural gas, coal, electricity, and renewables. U.S. crude oil and natural gas production trends, consumption, imports/exports, and storage are detailed. It also highlights prices for WTI, Brent, diesel, gasoline, natural gas, and electricity. The report includes GDP, manufacturing indices, degree days, and energy production/consumption by sector. Forecast adjustments are noted across time periods and fuels.
