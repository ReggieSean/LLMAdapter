This OCHA report analyzes funding gaps in humanitarian needs during the COVID-19 pandemic. It compares planned vs. funded requirements across sectors and countries, highlighting significant shortfalls in health, WASH, and protection. Key barriers include uneven funding distribution, insufficient flexible funding, and delayed disbursements. The analysis urges improved donor coordination, greater transparency, and increased multi-sectoral support. Recommendations target equitable funding across countries and sectors, emphasizing the need for rapid, predictable financing.
