This document provides detailed construction and health regulations for food service facilities. It covers equipment requirements (NSF standards), ventilation, plumbing, fire suppression, sanitation, ADA compliance, and material guidelines. Contractors must follow local, state, and federal codes, ensure proper installation, provide adequate lighting, drainage, and ventilation, and coordinate inspections. It also outlines electrical, mechanical, and plumbing specifics, including sink setups, floor finishes, backflow prevention, and fire sprinkler systems. Comprehensive signage, safety, and hygiene measures are emphasized for compliance and employee safety.
