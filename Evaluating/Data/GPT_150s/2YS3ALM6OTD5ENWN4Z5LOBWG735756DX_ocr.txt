This Forest Land Use Plan outlines a proposed timber sale (NC1395-D) in the Tanana Valley State Forest, 28 miles southeast of Delta Junction, Alaska. The sale targets 220,000 board feet of white spruce sawtimber on 25.6 acres. It includes road improvement, partial harvesting for regeneration, and environmental safeguards. Impacts on water, wildlife, and cultural resources are minimal. The sale supports local industry, forest regeneration, and sustained yield management. Public comments were solicited before final approval.
