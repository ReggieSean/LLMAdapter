This USDA market report from October 19, 2010, covers produce pricing in New York. It includes price ranges and demand/supply conditions for round white potatoes, various apple varieties (Red Delicious, Gala, McIntosh, etc.), yellow onions, and green cabbage. Prices were mostly steady, with light to moderate supply and fair demand across items. Movement to storage impacted availability, especially for potatoes. Occasional price fluctuations were noted for apples and onions.
