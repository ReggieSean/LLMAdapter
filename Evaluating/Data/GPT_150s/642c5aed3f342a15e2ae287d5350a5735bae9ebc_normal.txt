The OCHA Data Responsibility Guidelines offer principles, tools, and actions to ensure safe, ethical, and effective data management in humanitarian contexts. They guide OCHA staff in coordinating, analyzing, and sharing data while protecting privacy and minimizing harm. Key elements include data impact assessments, incident management protocols, and alignment with UN data strategies. The guidelines promote accountability, field-level implementation, and system-wide coordination across clusters and sectors, ensuring informed decision-making and safeguarding affected populations.
